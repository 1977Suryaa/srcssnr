Sri Ramakrishna Central School (SRCS) is a day school which is located in SNR Sons College Campus, Avarampalayam. SRCS is run by SNR SOMS CHARITABLE TRUST which is a well known organization providing medical and education services since 1970 at Coimbatore. SRCS follows CBSE curriculum to provide a holistic development and a practical learning experience to the young generation. To create a society where children are taught humane values in life. We have a team of dedicated and proficient teachers who treat the children with motherly care and compassion.
